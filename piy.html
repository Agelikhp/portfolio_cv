"PIY - Print it Yourself" - Silk Screen Printing Labs Exhibition, The Art Foundation Athens, Greece - June - July 2019.
PIY Project
Role/Contribution: Artist & Participant

Description: This exhibition presented my first line of silk screen printed t-shirts at The Art Foundation, showcasing my exploration of textile design through printmaking techniques. During this creative phase, I transitioned from doodling to producing prints, giving life to my sketches. I was particularly fascinated by how I could use the femininity inherent in my doodles to discuss themes surrounding the female body and its freedom.

Process: I developed designs based on personal themes and experimented with different printing techniques during workshops. Documentation included process photos and design iterations that captured the evolution of my work.

Outcome: The exhibition received positive feedback for its originality and craftsmanship, leading to discussions about sustainable fashion practices in contemporary art. I am grateful for the opportunity to explore new techniques and tools during this project, including gaining familiarity with wood workshops and large machines, as well as welding methods for constructing artworks. This experience enriched my artistic practice and deepened my understanding of textile design.
