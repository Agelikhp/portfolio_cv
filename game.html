Play Amygdala Hijack Adventure!


Amygdala Hijack Adventure is an ongoing project that aims to explore themes of emotional response and decision-making through interactive gameplay...

This project seeks to challenge players to confront their fears and anxieties in a safe space, encouraging them to reflect on their own emotional journeys... Sound Design: soundtracks of old memories, messed up, not in order, traumatic expreriences, party, overwelming experieces.... etc !!!!

Programming Languages used: HTML, CSS, JScript.
